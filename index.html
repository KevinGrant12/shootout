How to Win

Safely traverse the trail to the town Taco El Burrito

  1. Select Cards

    Select two cards and try to match them.

  2. Good Cards

    A match will move you to the next location on the trail. Matching good cards will help you with health and ammo.

  3. Bad Cards

    Matching bad cards will prevent losing health and ammo.
    Mismatching bad cards has consequences!
